You can play spatial videos in immersive mode with AVPlayerViewController. Since standard player by AVFoundation is supporting to play Spatial Video. Also another way is to use VideoPlayerComponent in RealityKit. VideoPlayerComponent would be easier way in Immersive Mode. https://developer.apple.com/documentation/realitykit/videoplayercomponent https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2024/10115 I guess spatial video in WebKit may be playable. it would be great that you can try it out. https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2024/10065 (edited)
